Chilled Cucumber Soup

Description

Description text here.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb english cucumber, rough chopped
  • 1/2 c butermilk
  • 1/4 c assorted herbs (mint, dill, basil, etc.)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 jalepeno

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Adjust seasoning to taste.
